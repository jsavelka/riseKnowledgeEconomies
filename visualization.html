Rise of Knowledge-based Economies

This visualization depicts the government expenditures per student, and research and development, as well as amount of internet users, per country. The graph below is sorted based on an aggregated total of all three variables. It ranks the countries based on their spending in these areas, which we believe are areas that contribute to the economy, which we call "Knowledge-based Economies". You can hover over the data or click on a piece of data to get more information. The dashboard that will appear contains more in-depth information regarding the actual spending amounts. The dashboard also provides the option of viewing a world map that displays the aggregate totals of each country, as well as the option to permanently color the selected path (for easier comparison of paths). To the right is a legend, which also allows you to control the amount of "weight" each variable has in the ranking process.
